Egg Omelette

French Fries

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Potatoes
  • Salt
  • MSG
  • Pepper powder
Instructions:
  1. Pre heat pan until smoking hot. Then turn down the heat to low.
  2. Add olive oil (NOTE: Hot pan plus cold oil prevents the eggs from sticking in the pan.)
  3. Add the whisked eggs in the pan. Make sure to scatter it around
  4. Add salt, MSG and pepper powder(optional) to taste.
  5. Wait 2-3 minuntes then flip the omelette.
  6. After 1-2 minutes, remove then serve immediately.
