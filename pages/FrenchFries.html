French Fries

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper powder
  • Mayo/Banana Ketchup (dip)
Instructions:
  1. Cut potato into fries cut.
  2. Either you can boil the potato (3-5 mins) or soak them into water for (10-20 mins).
  3. Drain and make the potato dry as possible.
  4. Pre heat pan and oil. High heat for pre heating and turn to medium low heat after putting the potato.
  5. Make sure to put enough oil to deep fry the potato.
  6. After 10-15 minutes remove the potato and put it to drainer. Let rest 3-5 minutes
  7. Time for second fry, make sure the oil and pan is hot.
  8. Use high heat and deep fry the potato for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Serve immediately with Mayo/Banana Ketchup
